Delivery Time

Requirements:

  • you'll have to use your own contract with the carrier
  • the sender has to be located in Germany
  • the recipient has to be located in one of the following countries:
    • Austria (AT)
    • Switzerland (CH)
    • Germany (DE)
    • Italy (IT)
    • Luxembourg (LU)
  • either time_of_day_earliest or time_of_day_latest MUST be specified
