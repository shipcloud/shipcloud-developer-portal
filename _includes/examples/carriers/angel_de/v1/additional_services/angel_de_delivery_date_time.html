MyTime

With the ANGEL MyTime service you can choose between several two-hour time frames for the delivery of your item. The following options are available for time_of_day_earliest and time_of_day_latest (Monday - Saturday).

In specific German conurbations for evening deliveries:

  • 18:00 - 20:00
  • 18:00 - 22:00
  • 19:00 - 21:00
  • 20:00 - 22:00
  • 21:00 - 23:00

Requirements:

  • carrier has to be angel_de
  • package.type has to be parcel
  • service has to be same_day
  • pickup is mandatory
  • to.email is mandatory
  • You'll have to use your own ANGEL contract.
  • Sender and recipient have to be located in Germany.
  • You'll need to provide a delivery date and a valid delivery time slot.

Notice:

  • When creating a shipment for ANGEL without specifying the additional service angel_de_delivery_date_time, the delivery time will be defined as being between 18:00 and 23:00
