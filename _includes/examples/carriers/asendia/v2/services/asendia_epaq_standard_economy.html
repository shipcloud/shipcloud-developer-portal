e-Paq Standard Economy

The service "e-Paq Standard Economy" is a standard service without tracking and without modifier to speed up shipping time for less urgent cross-border shipments.

Additional requirements (see global requirements):

  • Only applicable for shipments up to 2,000g
  • Maximum dimensions: longest side 600mm, length + width + thickness combined 900mm
