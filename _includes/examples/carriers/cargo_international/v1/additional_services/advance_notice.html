Appointment announcement

If you want the recipient to get a call from the carrier some time before the shipment will arrive at its destination you can specify the phone number of the recipient via our additional service advance_notice.

Requirements:

  • service has to be standard
  • package.type can be disposable_pallet, euro_pallet or cargo_international_large_parcel
