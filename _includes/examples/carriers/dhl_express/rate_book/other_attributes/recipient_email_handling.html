Recipient email handling

You can provide a recipients email address using the to.email attribute. When this is done and the contract being used has the ODD option (On Demand Delivery) activated, the carrier will contact the recipient via email before making a delivery.

Requirements:

  • service has to be one_day or one_day_early
  • you'll have to use your own contract with the carrier
  • the carrier contract needs to have the ODD option (On Demand Delivery)
